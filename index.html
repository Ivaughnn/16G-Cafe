Welcome to 16G Cafe, where every cup tells a story. Nestled in the heart of San Carlos City, Pangasinan, our cafe blends the aroma of freshly brewed coffee with a cozy ambiance perfect for catching up with friends or unwinding alone. Whether you're here for our specialty roasted beans, artisanal pastries, or simply to enjoy a moment of tranquility, 16G Cafe offers a delightful escape from the bustle of everyday life. Join us and savor the essence of community and craftsmanship in every sip.

Some of our Products

At 16G Cafe, we pride ourselves on three standout features that set us apart. First, our hand-selected, ethically sourced coffee beans are roasted to perfection, ensuring every cup delivers a rich and robust flavor. Second, our in-house bakery crafts artisanal pastries daily, from flaky croissants to decadent muffins, using only the finest ingredients. Third, our signature blend of teas offers a refreshing alternative, with a variety of flavors that cater to every palate. Whether you're a coffee connoisseur, a pastry lover, or a tea enthusiast, 16G Cafe promises an exceptional experience with every visit.
